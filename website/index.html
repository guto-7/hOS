About

Built for private, on-device interpretation.

On-Device AI

A fine-tuned 7B language model runs entirely on your machine via llama.cpp. No internet required for interpretation. Your blood panel is analysed locally with structured clinical reasoning.

Interprets Your Panel

Upload a PDF from any pathology lab. hOS extracts your markers, identifies patterns across clinical conditions, and generates a structured analysis. Ask follow-up questions in chat.

Private by Design

Your health data never leaves your device. No cloud storage, no user accounts, no telemetry. The only optional network request is an anonymised literature search — and you control the toggle.

Quick answers

Questions about hOS

Does hOS run locally?

Yes. hOS is designed to run on your device, with on-device analysis as the default experience.

Does my bloodwork get uploaded to the cloud?

No. hOS is designed to keep your health data on your device. Optional network features should always be user-controlled.

What files can I upload?

Bloodwork PDF reports from pathology labs. The app extracts markers and structures them for interpretation.

Which platforms are supported?

hOS is intended for macOS, Windows, and Linux, depending on the builds you publish.

Is hOS a replacement for a doctor?

No. hOS is an interpretation and education tool. It should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice.
